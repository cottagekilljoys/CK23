Welcome to the website for the Women and Gender Studies Colloquium of the Class of 2023! We are a group of Women and Gender Studies students at the University of Colorado - Boulder seeking to bring an end to campus gender-based violence and sexual assault. To accomplish this, we created a community project dedicated to remembering our collective feminist histories and addressing gender-based violence and sexual assault, especially as it is perpetuated by Greek Life and the lack of adequate response by campus administration. Our project includes three major portions: The Public-Facing Address, The Archive, and Rupture and Disturbance. On this website, you can find our Manifesto which details our goals and demands for the university as well as our philosophy as feminist scholars. Additionally, we have Resources for information about gender-based violence and sexual assault on campus and ways to receive support. If you are interested in feminist histories, check out the Archive where we have chronicled Boulder feminist stories and histories.
