Welcome to a website-in-progress for the Women and Gender Studies Colloquium of the Class of 2023! We are a group of Women and Gender Studies students at the University of Colorado - Boulder seeking to bring an end to campus gender-based violence and sexual assault. On this website, you can find our Manifesto which details our goals and demands for the university as well as our philosophy as feminist scholars.
